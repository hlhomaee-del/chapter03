Practice questions with full rationales

Practice mode trains students to slow down just enough to understand the question stem before moving to answer choices. This is where the reading timer appears.

Why this practice works

The goal is not to read slowly forever. The goal is to notice whether you are actually reading the question stem or jumping straight to the answer choices. Many students lose points because they answer the topic they recognize, not the exact question being asked.

In this practice section, students first read the stem, then click to reveal answer choices. The platform records question-reading time, answer-decision time, and rationale-reading time. In the adaptive challenge and simulation test, all answer options appear immediately, just like a real test. Practice builds the habit; challenge and simulation test the habit.

Adaptive challenge

This mode selects weaker items first: missed questions, slow answer decisions, or questions with no practice history. Answer choices appear immediately. The rationale appears only after each item is submitted.

Simulation test

A test-style mode: no instant rationale. Submit all answers, then receive score, rationales, and a reflection prompt.
